What We Accept:

  • All seasons + sizes
  • Adult + kids clothing
  • Shoes, accessories, bags
  • Items with flaws (stains, etc)

What We Don't:

  • Underwear or socks
  • Toys, stuffed animals
  • Wet, moldy items

Our Mission

We're here to make a difference. Our sorting system ensures that every item finds its best possible future.

1. Re-Closet

Items in great condition are curated and sold in our shop, giving them a second life.

2. Re-Create

Clothing with minor flaws or unique potential is given to our talented Re-creators to be upcycled into new, one-of-a-kind pieces.

3. Re-Cycle

Items that can't be worn are sent to our textile recycling partners, keeping them out of the landfill.
